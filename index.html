Definition of Hacker:

Wikipedia define that Hacker is 'A computer hacker is any skilled computer expert that uses their technical knowledge to overcome a problem. While 'hacker' can refer to any skilled computer programmer, the term has become associated in popular culture with a 'security hacker', someone who, with their technical knowledge, uses bugs or exploits to break into computer systems.'

Types of Hacker:

There are many types of Hacker, but on this website, we're going to talk about 'Balck Hat', 'White Hat' and 'Grey Hat' Hackers. We can easily distinguish the differences between 'Black hat' and 'White hat' Hacker from how people named them. Briefly, black hat hacker is the bad guys who invade other people's system without permission for their purpose and a white hat Hacker is a group of people usually working for an organization to protect the security system. Besides, grey hat Hacker working similar way as black hat Hacker without malicious purpose.
